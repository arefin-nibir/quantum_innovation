Why Quantum Is Important

Quantum computing is a fundamentally new computational paradigm that enables breakthroughs across multiple domains by simulating complex quantum systems, optimizing large-scale processes, and enabling new materials and chemical discoveries.

  • Healthcare: Accelerate drug discovery and molecular modelling for targeted therapies.
  • Energy & Materials: Design catalysts and materials for efficient H2 production and CO2 capture.
  • Optimization: Solve large-scale logistical and operational problems for industry.
  • Security & Cryptography: Prepare for and protect against post-quantum threats.
  • Scientific Discovery: Unlock new insights by simulating quantum phenomena directly.
